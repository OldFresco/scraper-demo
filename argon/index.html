Likely Questions

Q. How much can I borrow?

A.We offer loans from £1,000 up to £5,000 in £1 increments. Before applying for a loan please consider carefully your personal circumstance. Ensure you are clear on the repayment terms and confident you can repay the loan.

Q. Over what period can I repay?

A.The repayment terms available are 12, 18, 24, 30 or 36 months.

Q. Can I repay my loan early?

A.Yes, you can repay your loan in part or full at any time. We do not charge an early repayment fee but will apply interest as permitted under The Consumer Credit (Early Settlement) Regulations 2004. Please contact us and we will give you a settlement figure.

Q. Do you charge an arrangement fee?

A.No, we do not charge an arrangement fee.

Q. How do I apply for a Likely Loans Personal Loan?

A.Apply online using our simple application form and receive an instant decision. However, if you have any questions not covered in the FAQs please contact us. Please note that we are unable to take applications over the phone. 47.9% APR representative.

Q. What happens if I miss a payment?

A. If your circumstances change and you struggle to make the repayments you should contact us as soon as possible on 0208 080 6450. As a responsible lender we are committed to helping you regain control of your finances and to find a suitable solution. If you are late making any payments we may charge you interest on the overdue amount at the interest rate specified in the agreement from when payment was due until payment is received in full. If you are concerned about your financial situation you can get help at moneyadviceservice.org.uk

Q. Do you consider bad credit?

A. Yes, we will consider your application as long as you have some UK credit history and haven’t been made bankrupt in the last 12 months. Before applying for a loan please consider carefully your personal circumstance. Ensure you are clear on the repayment terms and confident you can repay the loan.

Q. Do I need to be a homeowner to apply for a personal loan?

A. No, as long as you are a UK resident it doesn’t matter whether you are a homeowner or a tenant.

Q. How do I make repayments?

A. Repayments are made monthly by Direct Debit from your nominated bank account.

Q. I have lost my Loan Agreement, can I get a copy?

A. All successful applicants will be emailed a full set of their documents which we recommend are saved for future reference. However, If you have lost or misplaced your Loan agreement you can request a copy by writing to us and enclosing a payment of £1, which represents the fee payable under the Consumer Credit Act 1974.
